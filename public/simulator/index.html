Before you simulate, know what this tool is (and isn’t)

Portfolio Time Travel illustrates hypothetical scenarios so you can explore how savings, withdrawals, and allocation choices interact. It does not account for your specific situation or offer investment advice.

  • Assumptions and default inputs are for demonstration only.
  • Calculations run in your browser. Avoid entering personal data and review the Labs Privacy Policy for how optional submissions are handled.
  • Decisions about real portfolios should be made with qualified professionals accountable for your capital.
