Divestment explorer

Public research for values-aligned screens

This workspace assembles open datasets, working papers, and prototype tooling to evaluate corporate conduct for exclusionary mandates. The goal is transparency first: anyone can inspect the assumptions, improve the classifications, or reuse the data.

What’s inside

Datasets & taxonomies

JSON and CSV resources cataloging corporate behaviour, conflict involvement, and boycott participation. Every field links back to a primary source or research note.

Open content folder → Report a correction

Submit updated intelligence, company reclassifications, or data quality concerns. We track every change so users can audit the provenance.

File an issue → Processing scripts

Python and TypeScript utilities for syncing third-party source lists, normalizing tickers, and generating visual summaries. Fork the tooling to extend your own research stack.
